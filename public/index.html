Funds, Orders, and Wishlist Orders

Current fund and order information, except for balance available, is drawn from Alma Analytics. Balance available for funds is drawn from the Alma Funds API. Wishlist orders and wishlist balance available are drawn from Airtable.

Actual spend represents expenditures in Alma (invoiced orders). Projected spend represents encumbered orders plus expenditures; for subscriptions, renewal date is used as a proxy for projected spend date. Proposed spend includes projected spend plus wishlist items not yet ordered.
